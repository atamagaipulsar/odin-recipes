Adobo

Description

Filipinos love this dish because it involves easy steps and few ingredients. Despite its simplicity, it has a rich and savory taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup white vinegar
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 bay lear
