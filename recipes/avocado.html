Avocado Milkshake

Creamy and refreshing avocado milkshake.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • 5 cubes ice
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 1/3 cups of milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Place avocado, ice, sugar, milk, lemon juice, and ice cream into a blender.
  2. Puree until smooth.
